DIETER RAMS: 10 PRINCIPLES


GOOD DESIGN IS INNOVATIVE

I agree that design is innovative. Technology especially is constantly changing and evolving. Graphic Design being a digital discipline allows that infinite expansion to exist.

GOOD DESIGN MAKES A PRODUCT USEFUL

I agree that design makes products useful. The whole progression, or mission if you will, of the design process is to create the most successful form of communication or use. wether that be from a 2D or 3D perspective.

GOOD DESIGN IS AESTHETIC

I agree that design is aesthetic. In considering the fundamental necessities of a successful composition, holding visual or conceptual interest is key. That can be through function of form or whatever may be considered “beautiful.”

GOOD DESIGN MAKES A PRODUCT UNDERSTANDABLE

I agree that design is understandable. Design is in itself a form of communication. Communication needs to be transferable and attainable by all who experience it. Just as the words and sentences of a novel need to be coherent and “self-explanatory” to the reader consuming that information.

GOOD DESIGN IS UNOBTRUSIVE

I agree that design is unobtrusive. This is one of the more prominent characteristics that separates design from just regular art. granted self expression can exist within a design, but this idea of neutrality within a tool and leaving room for self expression is what gives the design of a product so much variety and luxury.

GOOD DESIGN IS HONEST

I agree that design is honest. For successful design does not fantasize whatever is being communicated. Industrial design specifically contains this quality, graphic design can have more experimental and flexible parameters in relation to manipulation, but honesty still exists within the intent of a 2D composition.

GOOD DESIGN IS LONG-LASTING

I agree that design is long-lasting. The relationship that design needs to have and maintain with society is what gives it that long lasting life. Creating a transferable, almost eternal entity in reality.

GOOD DESIGN IS THOROUGH DOWN TO THE LAST DETAIL

I agree that design is thorough. care and accuracy is one of the key design processes. Considering every which was your design can exist and be perceived through the process of experimentation, and constant citique. Design is so thorough, “perfection” does not exist within any composition or product. It can always be better.

GOOD DESIGN IS ENVIRONMENTALLY-FRIENDLY

I agree that design is environmentally friendly. In understanding the responsibility that design has in improving our world/reality, acknowledging the preservation of our planet is a no brainer. Designers have expectations to meet in regards to the wellbeing of our planet just as much as the rest of mankind.

GOOD DESIGN IS AS LITTLE DESIGN AS POSSIBLE

I agree that design is simplistic. The goal in communicating through the design process is to be as exact and concise as possible. Communication through the full understanding of what is essential and non-essential.
